01 — Profile

About Me

I'm a Final-year B.Tech student in ECE (VLSI) at SR University, Warangal, holding a 9.077 CGPA and consistently ranked as Semester Topper. My research focuses on FinFET device reliability, low-power CMOS circuit design, and hardware cryptography using HSPICE and LTspice.

Over two years I've contributed to 10+ papers accepted or published at IEEE conferences (ICDCS, CICT, INSPECT, IATMSI), won the Best Paper Award at IEEE ICDCS 2026, and represented my department on a fully JST-funded research trip to Hiroshima University, Japan through the Sakura Science Exchange Program — including a visit to Micron Technology's Fab 15.

Beyond research, I serve as General Secretary of the Student Council at SR University, hold a Samsung India Fellowship (ISWDP Cohort 6), and recently joined as Campus Ambassador for E-Cell, IIT Bombay.
